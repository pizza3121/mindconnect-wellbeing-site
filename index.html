About MindConnect

A student built signposting hub for finding support sooner

MindConnect is a UWS London Campus student wellbeing project. It does not replace professional help; it brings trusted campus, NHS and charity signposting into one clear place.

Why it exists

Students can lose time searching across different services when they already feel under pressure.

What it does

It groups support routes, urgent contacts, self help guides and project evidence in a simple, assessment ready website.

🌿 Anxiety & stress

Anxiety is common and treatable. These tools help in the moment and over time.

  • NHS Every Mind Matters: free anxiety and stress tools nhs.uk
  • Anxiety UK: helpline and self help anxietyuk.org.uk
  • Try the 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 grounding technique in the self help guides.
  • UWS Wellbeing Service: book a 1:1 wellbeing appointment.

Last checked: June 2026

🌤️ Low mood & depression

  • NHS: depression self assessment and talking therapies self referral nhs.uk
  • Mind: understanding depression mind.org.uk
  • Student Space by Student Minds: text, web chat and phone support studentspace.org.uk

Last checked: June 2026

🌙 Sleep

  • NHS: how to fall asleep faster and sleep better nhs.uk
  • Sleepstation and Sleepio: CBT for insomnia, subject to NHS access.
  • See Guide 3 for a simple wind down routine.

Last checked: June 2026

📚 Academic pressure

  • UWS Effective Learning Service: study skills and time management.
  • Talk to your Personal Tutor early. Extensions and ECS are there to help.
  • See Guide 4 on managing deadlines without burning out.

Last checked: June 2026

💷 Money worries

  • UWS Funded Student Support and Hardship Fund: ask the finance team.
  • Blackbullion: free student budgeting tools blackbullion.com
  • Citizens Advice: free, confidential money and debt advice.

Last checked: June 2026

🆘 Urgent & crisis help

  • Emergency: if life is at risk, call 999.
  • Samaritans are free, 24/7: call 116 123 or email jo@samaritans.org.
  • Shout offers 24/7 text support: text SHOUT to 85258.
  • NHS 111: select the mental health option for urgent help.

Last checked: June 2026

About the project

Built by students, for students

MindConnect is a voluntary project by six MSc Project Management students at UWS London Campus, created for the Student Wellbeing Service. We don’t replace professional help. We make it easier to find.
